Promote access to education through educational scholarships for children in rural communities.

Mobile Library Project is an initiative of Boa Daakye Foundation on a mission to improve literacy in children and help groom them for the future. Our vision is to create a world where every child has access to quality education and grows up to turn the tide of poverty around.

Our Goals

We are focused on helping to achieve the Sustainable Development Goal 4 and 5, quality education and gender equality respectively.

  • Improve literacy skills at all levels.

  • Ensure all children access basic education and literacy skills.

  • Sustain and promote development through literacy.

Initiatives under the mobile library project include: the Mobile lab; an I.T inclined project that provides opportunity for learners to leverage technological devices (tablet, laptop, Rachel device) to learn, 2. Kasatech: This project allows leaners to learn different languages while enriching their literacy skills. 3. Festive Fiesta: Our yearly event held every December. We celebrate Christmas with kids and provide opportunities for them to learn. 4. Read to Heal: Read to heal is an initiative by the Mobile Library Project under the auspices of Boa Daakye Foundation which aims at promoting literacy in children at various hospitals in the Central Region of Ghana, 5. Holiday Drive: On holidays while most kids are at home and free, we run our Reading Clinics with beneficiaries and engage them in activities aimed at promoting literacy and 6. Story Podcast: Get the opportunity to listen to audio stories of different genre at anywhere and at any time.
